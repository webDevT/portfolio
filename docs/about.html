About me

My background

I'm a Product Designer based in London - UK - with over ten years of experience helping companies of all needs and sizes (from start-ups to big corporations) to innovate through design.

Having acquired a multidisciplinary profile through my career, I proactively have been involved along all product development lifecycle ;during conceptualisation - user research, workshops -, crafting - branding, UX, interface design, prototyping - and delivering - developers handover, quality assurance-.

Being able to interact with all the stakeholders and users of the project, I always found myself in a pivoting position where I collaborate with the different involved parts (final users, project managers, product owners, developers, clients...), learning from every one of them and (hopefully!) teaching them the importance to advocate for the final user when taking decisions.

I'm currently working for the London based Digital Agency ROKi Ltd as a UX/UI Product Designer.
