Gathering information
Brief and Brand Guidelines

The first step at every design project is to gather all the information the client currently has and to understand the brief. This includes Research up to date, Product Strategy and Brand Guidelines.

Requirements
User Flows

First, we used information architecture to help us build the user stories that were needed to be accomplished by the user. Once we had all the actions listed, we moved into building the user flows where we brokedown step by step what was needed to carry out the actions previously listed. This stage in the app or web development alignseveryone to hit the brief goal and lock the design job that needs to be carried out before starting applying the design.

UI
Applying brand guidelines to the design

After we gathered all the requirements and defined all the user flows, we started applying the design onto the wireframes we defined during the Requirement stage.

Protoyping
Validating the Design

We tested every flow and feature by building a Figma prototype before doing the handoff to the developers. These were used to test with real users to check the overall Look & Feel, user pain points and App performance. We needed 3 iterations in total before handing off the design to the developers.
