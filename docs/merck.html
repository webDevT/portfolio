Brand Guidelines
Gathering information

As a standard part of the UX process, we created information architecture before building any app screen. Organizing each of the paths through a visual hierarchy for every product the bank wanted to offer (Credit, Debit and Saving Pots) and creating a legend for displaying different types of features, interactions, and flows helped us to limit and size the number of them and understand how many flows and which features to include in them.

Protoyping
Speeding up the process by assuring consistency in our designs

We maintained a consistent design language along the Onmo App by building a Design System right from the beginning. Our foundations supported custom made icons, grid systems, spacing scale, typography scales and color systems. The chosen colour scale allowed for a branded palette that was flexible with light and dark color variations while we maintained a global interactive, neutral feeling.

This allowed us a safe quick build up of the previous flows we already agreed with the client and the capabilities to for more flexibility in element placement on pages with a wider range of background options while maintaining optimial contrast.
